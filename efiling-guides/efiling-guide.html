SARS eFiling Guide – 2025

Step 1 – Register on eFiling

  1. Go to www.sars.gov.za → click eFiling
  2. Click Register → choose Individual
  3. Enter ID number, tax reference (if any), contact details
  4. Create username / password
  5. Activate with PIN sent via SMS or email

Step 2 – Collect Your Documents

  • IRP5 from employer
  • IT3(a) – interest certificates
  • IT3(b) – dividend certificates
  • Medical-aid certificate
  • Retirement-fund certificates (pension, provident, RA)
  • Rental income & expense records
  • Investment statements
  • Section 18A donation receipts

Step 3 – Log in & Start Return

  1. Log into eFiling
  2. Menu → Returns → Income Tax Return (ITR12)
  3. Select 2025 tax year
  4. Click Create Return

Step 4 – Fill in Each Section

  • Personal info: check & update
  • Income: salary, interest, dividends, rental, business
  • Deductions: retirement, medical, donations, home-office, business
  • Credits: medical-aid, additional medical, foreign tax

Step 5 – Review & Submit

  1. Review every section
  2. Check the auto-calculated tax / refund
  3. eFiling validates for errors
  4. Click Submit when satisfied
  5. Save / print confirmation

Step 6 – After Submission

  • SARS processes within ± 21 days
  • SMS / email when assessment ready
  • Log in to view Notice of Assessment
  • Refunds → bank account within 72 h
  • Tax due → pay before due date to avoid penalties
Key deadlines 2025
eFiling individual returns: 31 Oct 2025
Manual returns: 31 Jul 2025
Provisional taxpayers: 31 Jan 2026
